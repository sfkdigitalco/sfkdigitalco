// FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build my website?
+
Most websites are delivered within 7–14 business days from the discovery call. Smaller sites often come in closer to 7 days. We'll give you a specific timeline after the first call.
Do I need to provide content and images?
+
It helps if you have your logo and a general idea of what you want to say. We'll handle the layout, structure, and design. If you have photos, we'll use them — if not, we'll use professional stock imagery.
What's included in the $97.99/mo management plan?
+
24/7 availability to report issues, same-day bug fixes, content updates, security patches, performance monitoring, uptime checks, and a monthly analytics report. Someone is always in your corner.
Can I cancel the management subscription?
+
Yes — cancel any time, no penalty, no fine print. You keep your website regardless. The management plan is month-to-month and completely optional.
Will I own my website after it's built?
+
Absolutely. Once the project is complete and payment is made, you own 100% of the website — all code, all files, all content. We don't lock you in or hold your site hostage.
What if I need changes after launch?
+
Management subscribers get unlimited content updates included. Non-subscribers can request changes at an hourly rate, or upgrade to the management plan for flat-rate peace of mind.
